REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Campus-Wide Network Infrastructure & Digital Learning Platform

Issued By: Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT-M)
RFP Reference: IITM/ICT/2026/017
Issue Date: March 12, 2026
Submission Deadline: May 5, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
Indian Institute of Technology Madras invites proposals from qualified networking and IT solutions providers for the complete overhaul of campus-wide network infrastructure and deployment of an integrated digital learning platform to support 15,000+ students, 800+ faculty, and 3,000+ staff across its 620-acre campus.

2. PROJECT SCOPE
The selected vendor will implement:
- Network Infrastructure:
  * Campus backbone upgrade to 400 Gbps fiber optic
  * Wi-Fi 7 deployment across 200+ buildings (4,000+ access points)
  * Data center upgrade (500 rack capacity, Tier-3 design)
  * Software-Defined Networking (SDN) with centralized management
  * Network segmentation for research, academic, hostel, and guest networks
  * Redundant internet connectivity (2 x 100 Gbps links)
  * Edge computing nodes for research labs (20 locations)
- Digital Learning Platform:
  * Learning Management System (LMS) for 800+ courses
  * Lecture capture and streaming system (150 smart classrooms)
  * Virtual lab environment for engineering simulations
  * AI-powered student analytics and early warning system
  * Plagiarism detection and academic integrity tools
  * Integration with NPTEL and SWAYAM national platforms
- Security Infrastructure:
  * Next-gen firewall with DDoS protection
  * Network Access Control (NAC) with 802.1X
  * SIEM solution for threat monitoring
  * Endpoint detection and response (EDR)
  * Compliance with CERT-In guidelines

3. CAMPUS STATISTICS
- Campus Area: 620 acres
- Buildings: 200+ (academic, research, residential, recreational)
- Students: 10,000 undergraduate + 5,000 postgraduate/PhD
- Faculty: 800+
- Research Labs: 350+ (including 15 Centers of Excellence)
- Current Network Users: 18,000+ concurrent at peak
- Data Generated: 50 TB/day (primarily from HPC and research)
- HPC Cluster: 2 PetaFLOPS (to be integrated)

4. CONTRACT DETAILS
- Contract Duration: 18 months implementation + 7 years AMC
- Estimated Project Value: INR 120 Crores ($14.4 million USD)
- Funding: MHRD grant (50%) + Institute funds (30%) + Industry sponsorship (20%)
- Location: IIT Madras Campus, Chennai, Tamil Nadu
- Commencement Date: July 1, 2026

5. COMMERCIAL MODEL
Bidders must submit:
a) CapEx: One-time cost for hardware, software, and implementation
b) Annual Maintenance: 7-year AMC with SLA commitments
c) License Model: Perpetual vs subscription comparison for all software
d) Buyback Value: For existing equipment being replaced
e) Energy Cost Projection: Estimated annual power consumption

- Minimum 8 years experience in large-scale network deployments
