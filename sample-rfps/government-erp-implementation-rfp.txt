REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System Implementation for State Government

Issued By: Department of Information Technology, Government of Maharashtra
RFP Reference: MAHA-IT/ERP/2026/003
Issue Date: March 15, 2026
Submission Deadline: June 1, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
The Department of Information Technology, Government of Maharashtra, invites proposals from qualified IT service providers for the implementation of a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system across 35 government departments and 200+ district/taluk offices. This initiative is part of the Maharashtra Digital Governance Mission 2030.

2. PROJECT SCOPE
The selected vendor will implement:
- Core ERP Modules:
  * Finance & Accounting (Budget management, treasury, expenditure tracking)
  * Human Resource Management (Payroll for 850,000+ government employees)
  * Procurement & Supply Chain (GeM integration, vendor management)
  * Asset Management (State property, vehicles, equipment tracking)
  * Project Management (Scheme monitoring, fund utilization tracking)
- Additional Modules:
  * Citizen Service Portal with 150+ e-services
  * Grievance Redressal Management System
  * Decision Support System with BI dashboards for Chief Secretary and Ministers
  * Document Management System with e-Office integration
  * Mobile application for field officers
- Infrastructure:
  * Cloud hosting on MeghRaj/GI Cloud or equivalent government cloud
  * Disaster Recovery setup
  * Data migration from 15+ legacy systems
  * Integration with Aadhaar, DigiLocker, PFMS, and other national platforms

3. GOVERNMENT STATISTICS
- Total Departments: 35 administrative departments
- District Offices: 36 district collectorates + 200+ sub-offices
- Government Employees: 850,000+ (target users)
- Current Systems: 15+ disconnected legacy applications
- Annual Budget: INR 5,00,000 Crores ($60 billion USD)
- Annual Procurement Volume: INR 45,000 Crores ($5.4 billion USD)

4. CONTRACT DETAILS
- Contract Duration: 3 years implementation + 5 years AMC
- Estimated Project Value: INR 650 Crores ($78 million USD)
- Funding: Central Government (40%) + State Budget (60%)
- Location: Mumbai (HQ), with pan-Maharashtra deployment
- Commencement Date: September 1, 2026

5. COMMERCIAL MODEL
Bidders must submit a commercial proposal including:
a) Implementation Cost: Phase-wise deployment cost
b) License Cost: Perpetual or subscription-based licensing
c) Annual Maintenance: Post-implementation support cost
d) Training Cost: Capacity building for 850,000+ users
e) Data Migration Cost: From legacy systems to new ERP
f) Cloud Hosting: Annual hosting charges

6. SCORING CRITERIA AND EVALUATION

6.1 Technical Solution (35 points)
- ERP platform and architecture: 12 points
- Customization and localization capability: 8 points
- Integration approach with national platforms: 8 points
- Data migration strategy: 7 points

6.2 Financial Proposal (25 points)
- Total cost of ownership (8-year): 15 points
- Commercial model flexibility: 5 points
- Value-added services at no extra cost: 5 points

6.3 Implementation Approach (20 points)
- Phase-wise rollout plan: 8 points
- Change management and training strategy: 7 points
- Risk mitigation and contingency planning: 5 points

6.4 Experience & Track Record (15 points)
- Government ERP implementations in India: 8 points
- Scale of past deployments (user base): 4 points
- Client references from state/central government: 3 points

6.5 Support & O&M (5 points)
- SLA framework: 3 points
- Helpdesk and support model: 2 points

Total: 100 points

7. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
- Minimum 10 years experience in ERP implementation
- At least 2 government ERP projects with 50,000+ users each
- Annual turnover of at least INR 500 Crores from IT services
- CMMI Level 5 certified
- ISO 27001 and ISO 20000 certified
- Empaneled with MeitY or any state government IT department
- Consortium permitted (max 2 members, lead must hold 60%+)

8. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
- Technical Proposal with solution architecture and deployment plan
- Financial Proposal with detailed cost breakdown (phase-wise)
- Proof of government ERP implementations (completion certificates)
- Prototype/demo of proposed ERP system
- Team structure with CVs of key personnel (minimum 50 resources)
- EMD of INR 10 Crores (Bank Guarantee)
- Compliance certificate for government procurement guidelines
