REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Smart City Integrated Command & Control Center with IoT Infrastructure

Issued By: Jaipur Smart City Limited (JSCL)
RFP Reference: JSCL/ICCC/2026/015
Issue Date: February 28, 2026
Submission Deadline: May 10, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
Jaipur Smart City Limited, a Special Purpose Vehicle under the Smart Cities Mission of the Government of India, invites proposals from qualified System Integrators for the design, development, implementation, and operation of an Integrated Command & Control Center (ICCC) with city-wide IoT sensor deployment for the Pink City, Jaipur.

2. PROJECT SCOPE
The selected vendor will be responsible for:
- Design and construction of a state-of-the-art ICCC facility (10,000 sq ft)
- Video wall and situation room setup (50+ display panels)
- City-wide IoT sensor deployment:
  * 2,500 smart streetlights with environmental sensors
  * 500 traffic monitoring cameras with AI-based analytics
  * 200 air quality monitoring stations
  * 150 smart parking sensors across key commercial areas
  * 100 water quality sensors on main pipelines
  * 50 flood detection sensors in low-lying areas
- Centralized data platform with real-time analytics
- Citizen engagement mobile application
- Emergency response management system
- Integration with existing traffic management, water supply, and solid waste systems
- 5-year Operations & Maintenance (O&M)

3. CITY STATISTICS
- City Population: 4.2 million (2025 census)
- Area: 485 sq km
- Tourist Footfall: 15 million annually
- Major Heritage Sites: 12 (UNESCO World Heritage - Jaipur City)
- Daily Vehicles on Road: 2.8 million
- Existing Smart Infrastructure: Partial CCTV network (800 cameras), GPS-tracked municipal fleet

4. CONTRACT DETAILS
- Contract Duration: 2 years implementation + 5 years O&M
- Estimated Project Value: INR 450 Crores ($54 million USD)
- Funding Source: Smart Cities Mission (60%) + State Government (25%) + Municipal (15%)
- Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan, India
- Commencement Date: July 15, 2026

5. COMMERCIAL MODEL
Bidders must submit a commercial proposal structured as:
a) Capital Expenditure (CapEx): One-time implementation cost including hardware, software, and civil works
b) Operational Expenditure (OpEx): Annual O&M cost for 5 years
c) Revenue Generation: Proposed model for generating revenue through smart advertising, data analytics services, and parking management
d) Performance-linked Deductions: Penalty framework for SLA breaches

6. SCORING CRITERIA AND EVALUATION

6.1 Technical Solution (35 points)
- ICCC architecture and technology stack: 10 points
- IoT platform scalability and interoperability: 10 points
- AI/ML analytics capabilities: 8 points
- Cybersecurity framework: 7 points

6.2 Financial Proposal (30 points)
- Total project cost (CapEx + OpEx): 15 points
- Revenue generation potential: 10 points
- Value engineering proposals: 5 points

6.3 Experience & Credentials (20 points)
- Smart city/ICCC project experience: 10 points
- Team qualifications and key personnel: 5 points
- References from government clients: 5 points

6.4 Implementation Approach (10 points)
- Project plan and timeline: 5 points
- Risk management and mitigation: 3 points
- Local workforce development plan: 2 points

6.5 Innovation & Sustainability (5 points)
- Green technology adoption: 3 points
- Heritage conservation integration: 2 points

Total: 100 points

7. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
- Minimum 3 Smart City/ICCC projects completed in India
- Annual turnover of at least INR 200 Crores in each of the last 3 financial years
- Consortium/JV permitted (maximum 3 members, lead must hold 51%+)
- CMMI Level 3 or above
- No blacklisting by any Central/State Government entity
- Net worth of at least INR 100 Crores

8. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
- Technical Proposal with detailed solution architecture
- Financial Proposal with CapEx/OpEx breakdown
- Consortium agreement (if applicable)
- Past performance certificates from at least 3 smart city projects
- Audited financial statements for last 5 years
- Manpower deployment plan with CVs of key personnel
- EMD of INR 5 Crores (Bank Guarantee)
