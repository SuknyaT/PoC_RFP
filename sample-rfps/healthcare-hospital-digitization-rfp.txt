REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Hospital Digital Transformation & Patient Information System

Issued By: Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Limited
RFP Reference: AHEL/IT/2026/042
Issue Date: March 10, 2026
Submission Deadline: May 20, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Limited invites proposals from qualified technology and healthcare IT companies for a comprehensive digital transformation initiative across 15 Apollo hospital facilities in South India. The project encompasses patient information display systems, digital wayfinding, telemedicine infrastructure, and health-awareness advertising networks.

2. PROJECT SCOPE
The selected vendor will be responsible for:
- Design, installation, and management of 800+ digital display screens across 15 hospitals
- Patient information display systems (queue management, doctor availability, lab results)
- Digital wayfinding kiosks with multilingual support (English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada)
- Telemedicine consultation room setup (50 rooms across facilities)
- Health-awareness and pharmaceutical advertising network
- Integration with existing Hospital Information System (HIS) and Electronic Medical Records (EMR)
- Mobile app integration for patient engagement
- Data analytics dashboard for hospital administrators

3. FACILITY STATISTICS
- Total Hospitals: 15 facilities across Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, and Telangana
- Annual Patient Footfall: 8.5 million OPD visits, 450,000 IPD admissions
- Daily Visitors (average): 25,000 across all facilities
- Staff Count: 12,000+ healthcare professionals
- Existing IT Infrastructure: Centralized HIS (Oracle-based), Wi-Fi 6 across all facilities

4. CONTRACT DETAILS
- Contract Duration: 5 years with 3-year extension option
- Estimated Project Value: INR 85 Crores ($10.2 million USD)
- Implementation Timeline: Phase 1 (6 hospitals) within 8 months, Phase 2 (remaining 9) within 18 months
- Location: South India (Chennai HQ)
- Commencement Date: August 1, 2026

5. REVENUE MODEL REQUIREMENTS
Bidders must submit a commercial proposal that includes:
a) Fixed Implementation Cost: One-time setup and installation charges
b) Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC): Ongoing support and maintenance fees
c) Revenue Share from Advertising: Percentage of advertising revenue shared with Apollo
d) SLA-linked Payment: Performance-based payment linked to uptime and service levels

6. SCORING CRITERIA AND EVALUATION

6.1 Technical Solution (30 points)
- System architecture and scalability: 10 points
- Integration capability with existing HIS/EMR: 10 points
- UI/UX design and patient experience: 5 points
- Cybersecurity and data privacy (HIPAA/DISHA compliance): 5 points

6.2 Financial Proposal (25 points)
- Total cost of ownership (5-year): 15 points
- Revenue share model: 10 points

6.3 Experience & Track Record (20 points)
- Healthcare IT project experience: 10 points
- Number of hospital deployments: 5 points
- Client references from healthcare sector: 5 points

6.4 Implementation Plan (15 points)
- Project timeline and milestones: 8 points
- Change management and training plan: 4 points
- Risk mitigation strategy: 3 points

6.5 Support & Maintenance (10 points)
- SLA commitments (99.9% uptime): 5 points
- Response time guarantees: 3 points
- Dedicated support team: 2 points

Total: 100 points

7. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
- Minimum 5 years experience in healthcare IT solutions
- Annual turnover of at least INR 25 Crores in each of the last 3 financial years
- ISO 27001 certified
- Experience with at least 3 hospital digital transformation projects
- HIPAA or equivalent healthcare data compliance certification

8. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
- Technical Proposal with solution architecture diagrams
- Financial Proposal with detailed cost breakdown
- Proof of healthcare IT certifications
- Case studies from minimum 3 similar deployments
- Team composition with relevant healthcare IT experience
- Data security and compliance documentation
