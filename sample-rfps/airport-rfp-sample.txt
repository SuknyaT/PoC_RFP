REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Airport Advertising Concession - Hyderabad International Airport

Issued By: GMR Hyderabad International Airport Limited
RFP Reference: GHIAL/ADV/2026/001
Issue Date: March 20, 2026
Submission Deadline: April 30, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
GMR Hyderabad International Airport Limited (GHIAL) invites proposals from qualified advertising companies for the exclusive right to manage, operate, and maintain advertising media across the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Hyderabad.

2. PROJECT SCOPE
The selected concessionaire will be responsible for:
- Management of all indoor and outdoor advertising spaces across Terminal 1 and Terminal 2
- Digital advertising displays (LED screens, digital kiosks, video walls)
- Static advertising panels (billboards, backlit displays, pillar wraps)
- Baggage claim area advertising
- Boarding gate lounge advertising
- Parking area and approach road advertising
- Total available advertising positions: 450+
- Contract includes rights to sell advertising space to third-party brands

3. AIRPORT STATISTICS
- Annual Passenger Traffic: 25 million passengers (2025)
- Projected Growth: 8% year-over-year
- International Passengers: 5.2 million annually
- Daily Flight Operations: 280+ flights
- Airlines Operating: 45+ domestic and international carriers
- Peak Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 AM, 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

4. CONTRACT DETAILS
- Contract Duration: 7 years (with option to extend by 3 years)
- Estimated Annual Revenue Potential: INR 150-200 Crores ($18-24 million USD)
- Location: Hyderabad, Telangana, India
- Commencement Date: July 1, 2026

5. REVENUE MODEL REQUIREMENTS
Bidders must submit a revenue model that includes:
a) Minimum Annual Guarantee (MAG): Fixed annual payment to GHIAL regardless of actual revenue
b) Revenue Share: Percentage of gross advertising revenue shared with GHIAL above the MAG
c) Annual Escalation: Proposed year-over-year increase in MAG

6. SCORING CRITERIA AND EVALUATION

Proposals will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

6.1 Financial Offer (40 points)
- Minimum Annual Guarantee amount: 20 points
- Revenue share percentage: 10 points
- Annual escalation rate: 10 points

6.2 Experience and Track Record (25 points)
- Years of experience in airport/transit advertising: 10 points
- Number of similar contracts managed: 8 points
- Client references and testimonials: 7 points

6.3 Technical Approach (20 points)
- Digital innovation and technology plan: 8 points
- Advertising inventory optimization strategy: 7 points
- Installation and maintenance methodology: 5 points

6.4 Organizational Capability (10 points)
- Team composition and key personnel: 5 points
- Financial stability of the organization: 5 points

6.5 Sustainability and CSR (5 points)
- Green advertising initiatives: 3 points
- Community engagement programs: 2 points

Total: 100 points

7. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
- Minimum 5 years of experience in advertising media management
- Annual turnover of at least INR 50 Crores in each of the last 3 financial years
- Experience managing advertising at minimum 2 airports or transit hubs
- No blacklisting by any government entity

8. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
- Technical Proposal (sealed envelope)
- Financial Proposal (separate sealed envelope)
- Company registration documents
- Audited financial statements for last 3 years
- Portfolio of similar projects
- Team CVs of key personnel
