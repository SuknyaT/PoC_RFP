REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
750 MW Solar Park - EPC & Operations Management

Issued By: Gujarat Urja Vikas Nigam Limited (GUVNL)
RFP Reference: GUVNL/SOLAR/2026/021
Issue Date: March 1, 2026
Submission Deadline: May 15, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
Gujarat Urja Vikas Nigam Limited invites proposals from experienced EPC contractors and renewable energy developers for the Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) of a 750 MW solar photovoltaic park in the Kutch district of Gujarat, along with 25 years of Operations & Maintenance (O&M).

2. PROJECT SCOPE
The selected contractor will be responsible for:
- Site Preparation:
  * Land leveling and grading across 3,500 acres
  * Boundary fencing and access road construction (25 km)
  * Storm water drainage system
- Solar Installation:
  * 1.5 million solar PV modules (bifacial, monocrystalline)
  * Single-axis solar tracker system
  * 150 central inverter stations (5 MW each)
  * DC/AC cabling and module mounting structures
- Electrical Infrastructure:
  * 5 x 220 kV pooling substations
  * 1 x 765 kV interconnection substation
  * 50 km HT transmission line to CTU grid
  * SCADA and remote monitoring system
- Battery Energy Storage System (BESS):
  * 200 MWh lithium-ion battery storage
  * Grid-scale battery management system
- Operations & Maintenance (25 years):
  * Module cleaning (robotic preferred)
  * Inverter and tracker maintenance
  * Vegetation management
  * Performance monitoring and optimization

3. PROJECT SITE DETAILS
- Location: Khavda, Kutch District, Gujarat
- Total Land Area: 3,500 acres (allocated by Gujarat government)
- Solar Irradiance: 5.8 kWh/m²/day (annual average)
- Temperature Range: 8°C to 48°C
- Nearest Grid Connection: 765 kV Bhuj substation (50 km)
- Water Source: Desalination plant (to be set up by GUVNL)
- Road Access: NH-341 (15 km from site)

4. CONTRACT DETAILS
- Contract Duration: 2 years EPC + 25 years O&M
- Estimated Project Value: INR 4,500 Crores ($540 million USD)
- Funding: Green Bonds (40%), DFI Loans (35%), Equity (25%)
- Power Purchase Agreement: 25 years with GUVNL at tariff discovered through bidding
- Commencement Date: August 1, 2026
- Commercial Operation Date (COD): July 31, 2028

5. COMMERCIAL MODEL
Bidders must submit:
a) EPC Cost: Lump sum turnkey price per MW installed
b) O&M Cost: Annual per MW charge for 25 years (with escalation formula)
c) Performance Guarantee: Minimum CUF commitment (≥23%)
d) Tariff Bid: Levelized cost of energy (LCOE) for PPA pricing
e) Liquidated Damages: For delays in COD and underperformance

6. SCORING CRITERIA AND EVALUATION

6.1 Financial Bid (40 points)
- EPC cost per MW: 15 points
- O&M cost (25-year NPV): 10 points
- Tariff bid (LCOE): 10 points
- Performance guarantee terms: 5 points

6.2 Technical Capability (25 points)
- Solar technology and module selection: 8 points
- BESS design and integration: 7 points
- Electrical infrastructure plan: 5 points
- O&M strategy and automation: 5 points

6.3 Experience & Track Record (20 points)
- MW capacity commissioned: 10 points
- Projects in similar terrain/climate: 5 points
- O&M track record and CUF achievements: 5 points

6.4 Project Execution Plan (10 points)
- Construction timeline and methodology: 5 points

- Risk mitigation (weather, supply chain): 2 points

6.5 Sustainability & Local Impact (5 points)
- Local manufacturing content: 2 points
- Workforce development plan: 2 points
- Environmental management: 1 point

Total: 100 points

7. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
- Minimum 500 MW of solar projects commissioned in India
- At least 1 project of 200+ MW capacity completed
- Annual turnover of at least INR 1,000 Crores from renewable energy EPC
- Net worth of at least INR 500 Crores
- No adverse performance record with SECI, NTPC, or state DISCOMs
- Must have in-house O&M capability or tie-up with rated O&M provider
- BIS certification for proposed solar modules

- Technical Proposal with detailed engineering drawings
- Financial Proposal with cost breakdown and tariff bid
- Module and inverter datasheets and certifications
